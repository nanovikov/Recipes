# Simeon Chicken

**Ingredients** 

* 2-4 chicken breasts
* Simeon's spice mix
* white wine
* lemon wedges

**Instructions**

1. Wash and dry chicken. Turn oven to 400F.
2. Dredge in Simeon's spice mix. 
3. Add to pyrex glass pan. Surround with lemon wedges.
4. Add white wine to the bottome on the pan (~1 mm). 
5. Wrap the chicken in parchment paper to create a steam bath. 
6. Bake 25 minutes until the chicken center is 160C. 
7. Rest 10 minutes to bake up to 165C. 
8. Enjoy.
