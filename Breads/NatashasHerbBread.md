# Natasha's Herb Bread

**Ingredients**

* 3/4 c milk
* 1 T sugar
* 1 t salt
* 2 T olive oil
* 1/4 c chopped onion
* 2 c bread flour
* 1/2 t basil
* 1/2 t rosemary
* 1 1/2 t Red Star active yeast. 

**Instructions**

1. Assemble ingredients in order in pan. 
2. Cook on regular setting with medium crust.
