# Doug's Toast Bread

**Ingredients**

* 3/8 c water
* 3/8 c milk
* 1 egg
* 1 T olive oil
* 2 T sugar
* 1 t salt
* 2 c bread flower
* 2 T wheat germ
* 1 T instant potato flakes
* 1 1/2 t Red Star active dry yeast

**Instructions**

1. Assemble in order in bread pan. 
2. Make on regular cycle, medium crust.
