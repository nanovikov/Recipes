# Cornbread

**Ingredients**
* 1 c (250mL) buttermilk
* 4 T butter

* 3/4 t salt
* 1 cup (140g) all-purpose flour
* 3/4 cup (120g) yellow cornmeal, preferably stone-ground
* 2 teaspoons baking powder, preferably aluminium-free
