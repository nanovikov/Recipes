# Sweet Potato Soup

**Ingredients**

* 2 lbs sweet potato
* 2 T butter
* 2 medium onions
* 2 c chicken broth
* 1 t ground ginger (or 3/4 inch fresh ginger)
* 1/2 t salt
* juice of 1/2 a lemon
* freshly ground pepper

**Instructions**

1. Cook potatoes. 
2. Saute onion. 
3. Add the rest of the ingredients to onions. 
4. Cool. 
5. Blend. 
6. Add milk.
